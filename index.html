About

A production company
built differently.

Mindzeye Films was founded in St. George's, Grenada in 2021 by Andre Hayes and Kate Masaltseva — an accounting and finance professional and a cinematographer, who met while studying in Shanghai and chose to build something in the Caribbean that the Caribbean didn't yet have.

We are a growing production company serving public sector clients, private enterprise, and international organisations across Grenada and the wider Caribbean. Our work spans documentary, commercial, institutional and social content — delivered with the discipline of a professional finance-managed operation and the creative ambition of a company that believes in what it makes.

We are based in St. George's, Grenada, with the capability to produce across the Caribbean region and internationally.
